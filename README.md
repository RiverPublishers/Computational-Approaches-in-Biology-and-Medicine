The notebooks are intended as **didactic companions**, not black-box analysis pipelines.

- Modify parameters or datasets to explore alternative scenarios,

- `numpy`, `pandas`, `scipy`
- `scikit-learn`
- `matplotlib`, `seaborn`

Where relevant, random seeds are fixed to ensure reproducibility.

## ğŸ” Reproducibility and Best Practices

This repository follows established best practices for reproducible computational research:

- Deterministic random seeds are used where applicable,
- Validation strategies (e.g., cross-validation, held-out test sets) are explicitly demonstrated,
- Synthetic or publicly available datasets are employed to ensure accessibility,
- Statistical assumptions and methodological limitations are discussed alongside results.

The goal is to promote transparent, interpretable, and reliable computational workflows aligned with modern biomedical research standards.
